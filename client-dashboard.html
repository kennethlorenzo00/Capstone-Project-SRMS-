Our Services


ANTI-MICROBIAL TESTING

This offers evaluating the efficacy of antimicrobial agents. It involves determining the minimum concentrations required to inhibit or kill microorganisms, helping to assess the effectiveness of antibiotics and other antimicrobial substances.

MICROBIOLOGICAL ASSAYS

This category offers techniques for examining and analysing microorganisms. This includes methods for counting microbial populations, identifying microbial species, and assessing their characteristics using various laboratory techniques.

LABORATORY EQUIPMENT USE

We also offer access to specialized laboratory facilities and equipment necessary for performing a range of scientific tests and analyses. This includes providing the tools and resources needed for microbiological assays and antimicrobial testing.
