Procedure Instructions

  • What is the procedure for sample submission?
    You need to fill out the submission form and attach your sample's information.
  • How do I know if my sample is submitted?
    You will receive a confirmation email once your sample is submitted.
  • What are the procedures for counting bacterial colonies?
    To count bacterial colonies, follow these steps:
    1. Prepare your agar plates and allow them to solidify.
    2. Inoculate the plates with the sample using sterile techniques.
    3. Incubate the plates at the appropriate temperature for a specified time (usually 24-48 hours).
    4. After incubation, examine the plates and count the number of colonies using a colony counter or manually.
    5. Record the results and calculate the concentration of bacteria in the original sample.
