Microbial Testing Methods

This offers evaluating the efficacy of antimicrobial agents. It involves determining the minimum concentrations required to inhibit or kill microorganisms, helping to assess the effectiveness of antibiotics and other antimicrobial substances.


  • Plate Count Method
  • Agar Disc Diffusion Method
  • Agar Well Diffusion Method
  • Minimum Inhibitory Concentration (MIC) Testing
  • Minimum Bactericidal Concentration (MBC) Testing

Microbial Analysis & Characterization

This category offers techniques for examining and analyzing microorganisms. This includes methods for counting microbial populations, identifying microbial species, and assessing their characteristics using various laboratory techniques.


  • Microbial Analysis of Water
  • Microbial Characterization
  • Microbial Culture Collections (PUPMCC)
  • Microscopy

ABOUT US

We are the Center for Natural Sciences and Resources Research at the Research Institute for Science and Technology (RIST), Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Our center is dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge and technological innovation through high-quality research and analysis.

Our Objective is to provide accurate, efficient, and reliable microbiological analysis services using the SmartColony application, ensuring high standards of research support for both internal and external clients.
