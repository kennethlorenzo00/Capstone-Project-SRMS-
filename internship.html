Internship Opportunity at the Center for Natural Sciences and Resources Research (CNSRR)

Why Join Our Internship Program?

Experience Real-World Research

Gain invaluable experience where you'll get hands-on experience with laboratory equipment and techniques that will prepare you for a professional career.

Research Mentorship

Work directly with experienced researchers, receiving one-on-one mentoring to sharpen your research skills and deepen your scientific understanding.

Collaborate on Meaningful Projects

Be part of research collaborations on impactful projects that aim to address key questions in microbiology and parasitology.

Who Could Apply?

We invite students with a passion for science and an interest in microbiology, parasitology, or STEM education. This program is perfect for:

  • Undergraduate students in Microbiology, Biology, Parasitology, or related fields.
  • Aspiring STEM educators looking to gain research experience.
  • Individuals aiming to pursue careers in research, diagnostics, or public health.

Apply Now!

To apply for the internship program, please submit your application along with a

brief personal statement outlining your interests and career goals.
